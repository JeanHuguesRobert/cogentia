# Democratic AI Safety — alias cogentia

The canonical Democratic AI Safety working paper now lives in the `barons-Mariani` repository:

https://github.com/JeanHuguesRobert/barons-Mariani/blob/main/research/democratic_ai_safety.md

This file is no longer a second version of the paper. It serves as an alias from the `cogentia` repository, so the corpus does not diverge.

## History

A parallel French draft was previously hosted at this path. It applied the Cogentia Commons format (Thesis Kernel + Objection Kernels + Coherence Kernel) to a related thesis — *Radical Democracy as the Best AI Safety Strategy*. It was a demonstration of the Cogentia Commons procedure on the foundation of the platform, before the platform existed.

In draft v0.5 (2026-05-18) of the canonical paper, the three substantive contributions of that parallel draft were merged into the main working paper:

- the comparative grid of AI safety strategies (now §3.5);
- the operational falsifiability conditions F1–F4 (integrated into §18);
- the Coherence Kernel articulating the relationship to DHITL (now §11.5).

The earlier French Cogentia-format draft is preserved in the git history of this file.
