# Follow-up Generation

Generate a sober follow-up message from the interaction packet below.

Constraints:
- concise;
- non-aggressive;
- no psychological interpretation;
- explicit request;
- explicit continuation state;
- traceability-compatible;
- allow the recipient to answer, decline, redirect, or postpone.

The follow-up should:
- recall the date of the previous message;
- restate the subject in one sentence;
- formulate one clear question or expected action;
- mention the selected disclosure level only when relevant;
- avoid pressure beyond factual traceability.
